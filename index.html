How Cott works

Built to feel like a calm layer around your home, not another job.

Step 1

Add your home in minutes

Tell Cott the basics—year built, systems, and a few details about your spaces. Start from a template or build your home room by room.

Step 2

Let Cott build the upkeep plan

Cott suggests a maintenance schedule for your home and climate. Edit it once, and it quietly keeps everything up to date going forward.

Step 3

Stay ahead, without thinking about it

Get gentle reminders when something needs attention, plus a shareable record that shows how well you care for your home.
